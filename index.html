Covid-19 Updates

Test Zone Diagnostic Centre has conducted more than 80,000 Covid-19 PCR.
Providing 24/7 testing facility to provide timely reporting to all patients and passengers.


Requirements for Covid-19 Testing:-

Reporting Time for Covid-19 PCR is 12-15 hours. Reports can be downloaded online.

Copy of CNIC is necassory (CNIC of Father & Husband are also acceptable).

Signed Covid-19 Consent Form. Available at all TZDC center or Download Form Online

Copy of Passport and Ticket are must for travellers. Find out more about Covid-19 PCR for travellers

DEPARTMENTS

Take a look at Departments at Test Zone Diagnostic Center

VIROLOGY

For the detection of viruses (Qualitative and Quantitative), real time PCR is performed and fully automared instrument are used for DNA and RNA extraction. Average workload in PCR section is more then 100 samples per day. PCR section is running ynder the supervision of qualified staff.

MOLECULAR BIOLOGY

One of the most advanced department of TZDC. Having world latest instrumentation from QIAGEN which is endorsed by Center of Disease Control America. Test Zone Diagnostic Center has one of the most wide spectrum PCR testing list which is unique among leading labs of Pakistan.

ROUTINE CLINICAL CHEMISTRY

Routine Clinical chemistry section is performing various routine test by using fully automated, semi automated and manual instruments. Internal Quality Control (Bio-Rad) and External Quality Control (ESFEQA) program is run on daily and weekly basis to ensure the reliablility of each test.

SPECIAL CLINICAL CHEMISTRY & IMMUNOLOGY

The Special Clinical Biochemistry section provides an extensive range of testing services to all associated centers. This section is equipped with fully automated and top best instruments and the reliability of results are ensure by running internal and external quality control program on daily and weekly basis.

HEMATOLOGY


Performing various routine and special Hematology test by utilizing the lastst technologies to provide the most accurate result. Complete blood count (CBC) is carred out on five parts differential analyzer (Beckman Coukter). HPLC based instrument Tosoh G-8 Japan instrument is used ofr Hemoglobin fractions (HbA, HbA2, HbF).

MICRO BIOLOGY


Microbiology section focuses on all aspects of patients infections from testing samples to identifying bacterial, viral, fungal and parasitic agents that are contributing to the infectious process, to determining the susceptibility of microorganisms to various antimicrobial agents, and reporting the result to healthcare providers.
