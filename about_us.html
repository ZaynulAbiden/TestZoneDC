About
Test Zone Diagnostic Centre

Since 2012, Test Zone Diagnostic Centre is one of the leading labs in Punjab with its vast network of collection points offering wide range of tests with prominence & quality assurance. Test Zone Diagnostic Center has been at the forefront of diagnostic healthcare in the region since it has been in operation. We are working under standardized and quality assured environment and providing our diagnostic services at affordable cost. Test Zone Diagnostic Center is dealing in fields of Biochemistry, Hematology, Immunology, Endocrinology, Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR), Microbiology and Virology. Test Zone Diagnostic Center has developed a specialized department for Covid-19 PCR where requirements of BioSafety Level-II has been achieved.
